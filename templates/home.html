ABOUT US

Welcome to Chromotherapy, an innovative mood regulation system brought to you by The Honourable Members. We are a team of 8 passionate individuals based in Durban, currently pursuing our bachelor's degrees in ICT. With a shared love for technology and software development, we’ve combined our skills to create something truly unique and impactful.

OUR MISSION

We aim to empower people by providing an accessible tool for emotional regulation. Mental health and well-being are often overlooked, but our web-based platform makes it easy for anyone to take control of their emotions in a natural, non-intrusive way. We believe that with the right tools, everyone can achieve better emotional balance.

WHAT WE DO

Chromotherapy is designed to help users manage their emotions through color therapy, powered by cutting-edge machine learning technology. Whether you’re feeling stressed, anxious, or just in need of a mood boost, our system suggests personalized colors that can help regulate how you feel. With just a few clicks, you’ll be on your way to a better, more balanced state of mind.

WHY IT MATTERS

Emotional regulation is a challenge for many people, and the usual routes—therapy, medication, or even self-help—can be costly or difficult to access. We aim to break those barriers with a system that is affordable, user-friendly, and effective. Chromotherapy isn’t just a product; it’s a step toward making emotional wellness available to everyone.
